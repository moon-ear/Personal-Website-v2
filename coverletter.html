Dear Shopifolk,

Although resumes can tell you a lot about an individual, they never tell you the journey that led the person to where they are today. My name is Munir Aljawahari. I am a student at the University of Ottawa, and I love using Web Development and my creativity to craft elegant user experiences.

The app may not have hit global charts, but it taught me so much about research, planning, designing, and crafting; traits that are valued at a place like Shopify.

This huge accomplishment pushed me to start attending hackathons, one of which I won and lead me to my first internship at Health Canada. The lack of software engineers at Health Canada forced me to look for ways to use software to better the team.
